## Usage Notes

- **Prefer compressed (webp) URLs** for faster page loads
- **Use original URLs** for high-DPI displays or hero sections where quality is critical
- **All URLs are tied to the website lifecycle** — they persist as long as the website exists
- **Text color over images:** Since most images have warm, light tones, use dark text (foreground color) for readability
