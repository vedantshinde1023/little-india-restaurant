### 1. Butter Chicken
- **Slug:** butter-chicken
- **Tagline:** Creamy tomato-based curry with tender chicken pieces—a timeless favorite
- **Price:** ₹499
- **What's Included:**
  - Tender chicken pieces in a rich, creamy tomato gravy
  - Aromatic spices and fenugreek leaves
  - Served with basmati rice or bread of choice
  - Perfectly balanced sweetness and tanginess
  - Slow-cooked for depth of flavor
- **Who It's For:** Perfect for those seeking comfort in a bowl. A gateway to Indian cuisine, loved by first-timers and connoisseurs alike.
- **FAQs:**
  - Q: Is Butter Chicken spicy? A: No, it's mild to medium with a creamy, slightly sweet profile. Adjust heat with our house chili oil.
  - Q: Can I get it vegetarian? A: Yes, we offer Paneer Butter Masala with the same rich sauce.
  - Q: What bread pairs best? A: Butter Naan or Basmati Rice complement the gravy beautifully.

### 4. Tandoori Chicken
- **Slug:** tandoori-chicken
- **Tagline:** Marinated in yogurt and spices, charred to perfection in the tandoor
- **Price:** ₹369
- **What's Included:**

### 7. Biryani (Chicken)
- **Slug:** chicken-biryani
- **Tagline:** Fragrant basmati rice layered with marinated chicken and aromatic spices
- **Price:** ₹449
- **What's Included:**
  - Basmati rice cooked with dum pukht method
  - Marinated chicken pieces
  - Layered with caramelized onions and mint
  - Served with raita and pickle
  - Complete meal in one dish
- **Who It's For:** For those seeking a complete, aromatic meal. Perfect for lunch or dinner.
- **FAQs:**
  - Q: What's the dum pukht method? A: It's a slow-cooking technique where rice and meat cook together in a sealed pot, trapping aromas.
  - Q: Is Biryani spicy? A: Mild to medium spice; we can adjust based on preference.
  - Q: Can I get it with mutton or prawns? A: Yes, we offer Mutton Biryani (₹599) and Tandoori Chicken Biryani (₹519).
